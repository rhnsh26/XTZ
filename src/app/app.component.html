Esports Media House.

XTZ eSports is a esport media house that focus on making esports mainstream in india. XTZ eSports is more than just a collection of teams, XTZ eSports is a portal into the complete world of eSports. XTZ eSports reaches the gamer market in a unique way, through the teams, and communities they love.


XTZ eSports also owns the team Particle 7.
